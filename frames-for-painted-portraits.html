Frames for Painted Portraits

  • Home
  • Frames

We transform your photo into painting on canvas art and frame it with wooden back. We offer frame with glass for charcoal drawings, pencil sketches and watercolor paintings.

Rolled in a Tube

Canvas shipped rolled in a tube, you arrange the framing at your convenience using your local framing store.


Galley Wrap

Canvas stretched over a wooden internal frame so can be hung without need for an extra frame. The edges are "over-painted" so that the painting wraps around the edge of the frame slightly, so the artwork is attractive from the side also.


NOTE: We DO NOT offer gallery wrap for

  • Charcoal drawings
  • Pencil sketches
  • Watercolor paintings
  • Pastel drawings
